Standard Units

The CompMath Unit

Targets: MS-DOS, OS/2, Win32


The CompMath unit provides procedures and functions to manipulate complex numbers, including standard arithmetic operations, relational operators and extended trigonometrically functions. Is also overloads the standard arithmetic and rationaloperators, and defines the system __reader and __writer functions for a Complex type.
