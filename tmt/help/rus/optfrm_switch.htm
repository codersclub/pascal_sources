$OPTFRM: Stack Frame Optimization Switch

Targets: MS-DOS, OS/2, Win32


Compiler directives

Switches on/off stack frame optimization.

Syntax: 
  {$OPTFRM+} or {$OPTFRM-}
Default: 
  {OPTFRM+}
