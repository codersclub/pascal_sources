== Group Members
=================

  GarbaGe is a group oriented to intro/demo coding. We are a new group
and we only know the demoscene for about 2 months.
These first releases won't be very good, (we're still learning a lot)
but I think we will get a lot better as time goes by...
  Right now, there are only two official members:

      eXtaCy - (aka XtaC/AD)

== Why GarbaGe ?
=================

  Hum... I thought about making this group while listening to
"I'm only happy when it rains" from Garbage, the music group.

start with GB_?????.ZIP. If you own a BBS and you are willing to
distribute our releases, please stick to these rules!
If you own a BBS and wish to be a DIST SITE for our group, then fill
the GARBAGE.APP file with BBS DIST APP on the OTHER [ ] option.
