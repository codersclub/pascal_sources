OVERVIEW
========
This archive includes pascal sources to program your VGA-video card
to show many different text video modes:
14, 15, 17, 22, 25, 29, 30, 34, 44, 50, 60 lines and
36, 40, 46, 70, 80, 90 columns in almost any combinations.
Also several demonstration programs included.
