Pager Plus -- Copyright (c) 1991 Prankware

Pager Plus was meant to be used with Galacticomm software BBSes.
You simply capture, with your term program, the list of users
currently on, by using the "#" command.  Scroll back or just
capture it halfway through and drop to a DOS shell.
From there, simply use the syntax:

page <infile> <outfile> [<message>]

...where <infile> is the file you captured to, <outfile> is the new
file you wish to write to, called the prank file, and [<message>]
is the optional message to go with the page, up to 60 characters long.

When you want to page everyone, which should be soon after you capture
the file, you simply type EXIT to exit the DOS shell, and do an ASCII
upload. See your term program documents for this. Enter the <outfile>
or prank file, and watch the pages fly through the screen...
You're bound to get many replies! 

Say "Hello!" to everyone, or be creative!
