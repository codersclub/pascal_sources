        have you ever played tic  tac toe?  all you have to
      do  is, first of all, choose between Xs or Os.  after
      that is  complete,  the player  that has  Xs  will go
      first.  the player will place the  X anywhere  on the
      3x3 grid.  then the O player will place an O anywhere
      on the  grid.  the object of this  game is to  make a
      line of 3-in-a-row on the grid. wether it be diagonal
      or straight.... so have fun!
