I got bored one afternoon and decided to mess around with accessing
information in ZIP and ARC files. ZIPPER.PAS contains some of the
more generic routines I wrote. If you are interested in writing some
of your own functions, please see the file APPNOTE.TXT that should
be contained in your copy PKZIP. Also, if you are interested in tackling
some of the more complex ZIP routines (LZW, Reduction, etc..) let me
know and maybe we can work on them together. Any questions, comments or
suggestions can be directed to me via easyplex or on Borlands CIS
confrence (A or B).  Messages left on BPROGB will actually end up
addressed to my good friend Greg Cassady, who will relay the message.
