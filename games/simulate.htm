 Aircraft Flight Simulator v.4.26 (640x400x16)  Thomas Brewster 21.08.1994

The sources for the article "Modelling Flight" by Thomas Brewster (April 1990 of _IEEE_potentials_). This program draw a pilot control panel that indicates all the flight parameters. Required: GRAPH.TPU and EGAVGA.BGI driver.
