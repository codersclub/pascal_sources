 Hackers on Night and Logic (English)   Pavel Anokhin 10.11.1997

Простая графическая игра в стиле Героев Магии и Меча II. В качестве объектов - компьютеры и хакеры. Английская версия.
Simple graphic game like Heroes of Might and Magic II, but with icons as object sprites.

                 Hi, All!

Once on the holidays I played in "Heroes of Might&Magic 2" 
and after I have bored, I think to write something in this style. 
As a graphics, I took icons of "Norton Commander", "Turbo Pascal" etc. 
This game is not large, but I beat my personal record, writting 77KB 
of the source in 3 days :). 
(yes, I have been making this game just 3 days and then never returned to it) 
If you like "HOM&M", you'll like this game, although it is simple. 
Unfortunately, some bugs were found in it - it's sometimes dropped 
to windows (in Win95) and sometimes hangs the computer. 
It's a cost of 77KB-in-three-days programming :-)
