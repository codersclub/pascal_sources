 Coder v2.3 - Simple Files Encoder for TP3.0  Luca Manunta 01.03.1991

Простой кодировщик файлов. Использует метод добавления некоего числа к коду каждого символа в зависимости от значения ключевой комбинации.
Coder v2.3 can be useful for all those who need minimal security on their own MS-DOS PC, to keep others from reading their texts or running "private" programs.



16k 
 
 I created this program in '89 with an old version of
Borland TurboPascal (3.0), so it may sometimes shows the "bugs"
of old age.

 It doesn't pretend to compare itself to Microsoft or Borland
software, I'm not a programmer, I just do that as a hobby during
my free time.

 Anyways, it can be useful for all those who need minimal security
on their own MS-DOS PC, to keep others from reading their texts or
running "private" programs.  I tried this program hundreds of times
and never had troubles.

Anyways, I won't take any responsibility for any loss of data that
may be caused from using it.

The zipped program must contain four files:

READ.ME  (this file)
VSCODE23.PAS (source file)
VSCODE23.COM (executable file with on-line help)
VSCODE23.000 (help overlay)
