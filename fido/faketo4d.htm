 FakeTo4D - Outbound Reconstructor for 4D login   Ron Pritchett 05.10.1991

This program will move and *.?lo files into a .PNT subdirectory of your outbound dir so that points can login with their 4D address. The mail packets themselves don't move & are still addressed to the pointnet address. Required: Turbo TechnoJocks Object Toolkit.

ok... FakeTo4D the 15 min solution for pointnet stuff <g>

This program will move and *.?lo files into a .PNT subdirectory of your
outbound dir so that points can login with their 4D address. The mail packets
themselves don't move & are still addressed to the pointnet address.

This thingy was really whipped together on the fly so I'll leave the docs
to an ablosute min! (I'll go through an exmaple, how's that?)

ok.... here's my setup:

me: 376/74 & our PointNet is 30122


376/16.5 is a point off another system in my net. Here's a walk-thru of
what would happen if I sent him netmail

1) I enter a msg in Maximus to 1:376/16.5
2) The message gets remmaped to 30122/5
3) The route file sends all mail to 30122/5 to 376/16

376/74.1 is a point off my system in my net. Here's a walk-thru of
what would happen if I sent him netmail

1) I enter a msg in Maximus to 1:376/74.1
2) The message gets remmaped to 30122/1
3) The route file holds all mail to 30122/1
4) Faketo4D sees a .HLO file for a pointnet address & moves the .HLO file
   into a sub dir off my outbound called 0178004A.PNT & erases the original
   .HLo file...   (btw 0178 is hex for 376 & 004A is hex for 74)
5) The mail is now waiting for 376/74.1 but the packet contains the
   address of 30122/1

well that's it boys & girls!
