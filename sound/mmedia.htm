 Multimedia System 1st Beta for TMT Pascal       Vadim Bodrov 25.03.2001


Multimedia System is based on Sound System Release 4 by Frontman of Crew242. Features: 4/8 & FX channel MOD-player, GUS, PAS+/16,SB/Pro/16,CD-Audio. Ideal for games & demos, 100% ASM, GUS/PAS/SB16 panning, 16 bit, interface units for TMT Pascal (MS-DOS 32-bit protected mode), interpolation, amplifying. The Source&Code is freeware!
