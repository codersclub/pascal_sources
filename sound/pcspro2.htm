 PC Sound Programmer v2.0 for PC Speaker  Foyal S.Carter 23.12.1994

Простой инструмент для создания программ, исполняющих тривиальные мелодии на PC Speaker. Вы задаете темп мелодии и ноты, а на выходе получаете паскальный исходник, готовый для компиляции.
"The PC Sound Programmer" v 2.0 Writes source code for Turbo Pascal Programs, Units, and Procedures to play PC speaker music. Freeware!

 If you typed "install" after unzipping this program, disregard
 this instruction: You may install this program anywhere you like
on your system without any problems. All you need are the
README.1ST, SOUND2.PAS and PCSTEMP.BAK files to make the program
operable. You may erase all the rest. They are intended primarily
for our friends, the Sysops.

 "The PC Sound Programmer" is a programming tool for the users of
Turbo Pascal. It is designed to greatly ease the pain of hand
coding source for melody lines by automatically doing frequency
and duration calculations and actually writing the source code
for you. With very few exceptions all displayed menus are
completely self explanatory.  The program takes a step by step
approach to coding your source.

 The first screen you will see displayed is, of course, a title
screen. (with only a 2.5 second delay)
