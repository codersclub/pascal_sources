 Background Mod Player v0.03 for Speaker, Covox, SB   Mark J Cox 03.11.1992

Обектный модуль MOD-OBJ.OBJ предназначен для проигрывания в бакграунде музыкальных MOD файлов на спикере, Ковоксе и Саунд Бластере. Приведены примерв использования на Паскале, С, Бейсике, а также схема для изготовления Стерео-Ковокса в домашних условиях.
The object file MOD-OBJ.OBJ that is part of this package allows programmers to add music and sound effects simply and easily to their programs. Music and sound effects can be played through a large number of sound devices, including the PC Speaker, the Soundblaster and various Covox devices. Music plays in the background.

     1. INTRODUCTION
     ---------------------------------------------------------

     The object file that is part of this package allows programmers
     to add music and sound effects simply and easily to their programs.

     Music and sound effects can be played through a large number of
     sound devices, including the PC Speaker, the Soundblaster and
     various Covox devices.

     Music plays in the background and there are a a huge number of
     ready-made music modules available on the Amiga and the PC (*).
