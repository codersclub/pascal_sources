"Адреналин 0.95" - Проигрыватель MOD/S3M файлов для GUS.
Adrenalin Module Player v0.95 by beta/a-men. requires: GUS & 750 kb XMS. features include:
- MOD & S3M support!
- dos-shell with vol bars onscreen
- file-selector & zip-support
- Turbo Pascal source
